Poema en inglés

Love, a force that knows no bounds, Unraveling the depths of our souls, In its embrace, we are found, Unique beings, with stories untold. A tapestry woven with vibrant hues, Each thread a tale of our existence, Love renders us whole, anew, Igniting passion, with no resistance. Words falter in capturing its essence, For love transcends mere language's reach, It's a symphony, a cosmic dance, A melody that words can never teach. It whispers in the gentlest of touches, In the tender gaze that speaks volumes, Love paints the canvas of our rushes, Expressing what words fail to consume. In the realm of love, we are set free, Boundaries dissolve, and spirits soar, For love is the language of eternity, Connecting souls, forevermore.

Diseñado con amor, por Villarroel Ruiz, Rudy; para: Olmos Alvarez, Daiana
